Biography

My name is Joan Ching Kwan Tam. I was born in Hong Kong on July 18, 1995 and moved to Canada on October 30, 1996. I have a dual citizenship as a Canadian citizen as well as a Hong Kong resident. I've lived in Calgary for most of my life in the NW section of the city, though I have moved houses 4 times. I went to a Catholic school from grade 1 to grade 12, graduating from Notre Dame High School in 2013. After high school, I entered the University of Calgary as an engineering student. I am now in my fourth year of Geomatics Engineering with a minor in Computer Science and am enjoying learning about the high tech side with topics such as satellite positoning and various GIS applications.
